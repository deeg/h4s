About Me.

Hard for a man with no head...

Long ago, before I can even remember, there was a tragic accident. The doctor was trying to remove some additional skin, when his hand slipped, and gouged right into my head. After this happened the doctors tried their hardest to save what was left, but there was nothing they could do.

Growing up without a head was difficult to say the least. Always struggling, I was never a good student and wasn't left with many options in my adult life. That's when I turned to CS:GO gambling which has been keeping my afloat for this long. I'm now turning to streaming to try to get some creditors off my back...
